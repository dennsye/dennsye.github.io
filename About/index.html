ABOUT ME

Hello! I am Dennyse Baja and I am currently a senior attending James Campbell High School. I was born in Cebu, Philippines but moved to Hawai'i when I was 4 months old.

For most of my life, I had always looked up towards the night sky and into the depths of the ocean below. Science had always interested and influenced me, whether it be astronomy & astrophysics or marine science. I recently however, have been more interested in medical and environmental science.

Some of my interests include music, watching movies & TV shows (especially in the supernatural or murder mystery/thriller genres), traveling, and playing video games.

I also have a soft spot for animals! I have three dogs: Curly, Yuda, & Uli. Curly is a shih-poo (shihtzu-poodle) & is my first & oldest dog! She loves belly rubs, eating, and sleeping on my bed. Yuda & Uli are both terrier mixes and are brothers! They originally were runaways who somehow found their way into my neighborhood last year on Thanksgiving. I led them to the backyard to keep them safe and called the Humane Society the day after. We eventually adopted them since no one claimed them ♡

My post-secondary education goals are to attend college at UH Manoa or HPU to major in Nursing while minoring in either astronomy, marine biology, or environmental science.
